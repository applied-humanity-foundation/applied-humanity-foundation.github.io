# applied-humanity-foundation.github.io

Official website of **Applied Humanity Foundation** — a Colorado nonprofit research and technology organization advancing human well-being through responsible AI application, open-source tools, and digital literacy education.

## About

Applied Humanity Foundation develops public-interest software, educational initiatives, research tools, and open technological resources using existing publicly available AI systems and APIs. We focus on application-layer innovation, human-centered technology, accessibility, education, and beneficial digital infrastructure that supports society.
